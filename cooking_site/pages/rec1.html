Tomato-Butter Roast Chicken

This is a Tomato-Butter Roast Chicken recipe

The Steps to making Tomato-Butter Roast Chicken

  1. season the Chicken
  2. Roast the chicken
  3. make tomato souce
  4. add butter to the tomato souce
  5. add the tomato butter souce to the chicken

ingredints

  • 1.5 kilo of tomato
  • 1.2 kilo of chicken
  • 1 spoon of butter
