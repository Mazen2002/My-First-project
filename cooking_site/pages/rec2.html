Chicken Alfredo

This is a Chicken Alfredo recipe

The steps to making Chicken Alfredo

  1. make white souce
  2. make the pasta that you want
  3. add the pasta to the white souce
  4. roast chopped chicken breasts (the size is up to you)
  5. add the chicken breasts to the white souce asswell

ingredints

  • 400 ml of creem
  • 50 grams of cheese
  • 400 grams of pasta
  • 500 grams of chicken breasts
